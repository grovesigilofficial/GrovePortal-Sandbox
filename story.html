★ MY AWAKENING ★

I didn’t plan any of it. One night when I was eighteen, I took something my friends called “sassafras,” and I believed them...

Looking back, I now know it probably wasn’t real sassafras at all. What I experienced aligns much more with MDMA or MDA...

The table started breathing. Textures moved like the world was made of soft waves. The room felt alive in a way I didn’t have words for.

I wasn’t spiritual yet. I didn’t know meditation, ego death, chakras, none of it...

And then came the breakup. Hard. Sudden. Like someone knocked the last support beam out from under me...
